Stir fried water spinach is a common Asian vegetable dish of stir-fried water spinach (Ipomoea aquatica). It is a popular Asian vegetable dish, commonly found throughout East and Southeast Asia; from Sichuan and Cantonese cuisine in China,[1] to Vietnamese, Malaysian, Singaporean and Indonesian cuisine. As a result, it is known by many names.
